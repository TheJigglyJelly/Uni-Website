    What is Cyber Security?

According to the National Cyber Security Center, Cyber Security is "how individuals and companies reduce the risk of a cyber-attack". This means that cyber is, in basic terms, a method of protecting yourself and others from all types of cyber-crime, from ransomware to trojans.
